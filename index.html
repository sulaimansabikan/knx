Your Gateway to Smart Building Technology

The KNX Basic Course is your essential first step towards becoming a certified KNX Partner. Delivered by KNX Certified Tutors, this course follows a standardized, high-quality curriculum set by the KNX Association.

Gain a comprehensive understanding of the KNX system through a balanced mix of theoretical knowledge and practical, hands-on workshops using ETS6 software.

Quick Course Details:

  • Duration: 30 hours (including final exams).
  • Prerequisite: None (Basic electrical/Windows knowledge recommended).
  • Certification: Official KNX Partner Certificate (min 50% passing mark).

Course Modules & Benefits

  • System Fundamentals: Understand the KNX system overview, topology (TP1, RF, Powerline), and communication telegrams.
  • ETS Project Design: Learn to design and structure a KNX project using ETS6 Professional, from basic to advanced levels.
  • Bus Devices & Installation: Identify KNX bus devices, understand their parameters, and learn professional TP installation rules.
  • Commissioning & Diagnostics: Master the skills to commission a KNX system and use diagnostic tools for effective troubleshooting.
  • No Prerequisites: Open to all, including electricians, system integrators, architects, and newcomers.
  • Career Advancement: Receive an official KNX Partner certificate and earn credits in the KNX Partner Ranking upon passing exams.
  • Free ETS Lite Licence: Participants receive a free ETS Lite license to practice and hone skills post-course.

Your journey begins online and completes with our hands-on expertise:

  1. Step 1: KNX ETS eCampus
